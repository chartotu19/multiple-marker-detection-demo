Multiple Marker Detection and Overlay Demo

This is a work in progress. A small app which uses JSARToolkit, Three.JS, Webrtc to detect multiple markers. You can find 1 marker here, multiple marker here. As soon as the app detects a marker, a sphere is overlaid with either red or green color in an alternating fashion.

Use a phone or tablet or another laptop screen or printout of the markers to test it. Demo Video coming soon for those who are unable to test it for some reason.
